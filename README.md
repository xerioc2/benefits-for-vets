# BenefitsForVets

A privacy-first, anonymous tool to help veterans discover benefits they may be eligible for.

## MVP Scope

The MVP allows a U.S. veteran to:
- Select their state
- Select their VA disability rating
- Instantly view a list of federal and state benefits they may be eligible for
- See why they qualify for each benefit
- Navigate to official sources for next steps

The MVP does not require account creation, login, or storage of personal data.
